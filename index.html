Interests

Given my interest and work in providing accurate and accessible information, my current goal for post-graduation is to work as a software developer for blockchain implementation. With this job, I am looking to build a location data system using blockchain to address the issues revolving around misinformation. I aim to solve such issues by working on developing decentralized applications with blockchain, which ensures immutability, transparency, traceability of application systems.

The concept of the Internet as a Place is what drives me as a researcher. The physicality of the Internet is a key that allows our life to expand further and provides innovation to the future generation. I am glad to be involved in the change and am excited to see what GIS brings to our life.

Apart from being a programmer and a GIScientist, I enjoy most of my time playing badminton. I have been an active member of the University badminton team, and winning a competitive tournament is my current goal.

When forced indoors, I follow several thriller and horror genre movies and drama series, I am an aspiring chef, and I spend a large amount of my free time exploring the latest technology advancements in the information architechture and web development world.
